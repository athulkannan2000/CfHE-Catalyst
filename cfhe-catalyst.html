ABOUT CfHE CATALYST

CfHE Catalyst is the product realization arm of the Foundation for the Center for Healthcare Entrepreneurship (CfHE), IIT Hyderabad. We provide world-class infrastructure and technical expertise to healthcare innovators, startups, and enterprises.

Our mission is to accelerate the journey from innovation to impact by removing technical barriers and providing access to state-of-the-art manufacturing capabilities.
